Timeline of APJ Abdul Kalam's Life

  • 1931 : Abdul Kalam was born as Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam

  • 1969 : Kalam was transferred to ISRO as the project director of India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III) to deploy the Rohini satellite in the earth's orbit

  • 1981 : Kalam got honoured by the Government of India with the Padma Bhushan

  • 1990 : Kalam got honoured by the Government of India with the Padma Vibhushan

  • 1997 : He has been honoured with the Bharat Ratna

  • At Defense Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), Kalam designed a small helicopter for the Indian army

  • 2002-07 : He served as the 11th president of India

  • 2009 : Kalam became the first Asian to be honoured with the Hoover Medal which is America's top engineering prize. He has also received honorrary doctorates from more than 30 universities
